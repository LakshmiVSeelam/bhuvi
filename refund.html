Refund and Cancellation Policy

Any refund on services you subscribe through Bhuvi Realtech will be governed by the Refund Policy enumerated below.

Cancellation and Refunds

  • Customer can place cancellation request and seek refund with in 24 hours from payment towards the services opted. Cancellation requests can be sent to the company email id customercare@bhuvirealtech.co.in, with Subject as Cancellation Request and clearly mentioning the customer id, property id and payment details in the body of the email.
  • Customer can place cancellation request after 24 hours from payment but within 15 days subject to acceptance by the company and will be eligible for only 50% of the subscription amount.
  • Customer will be eligible for refund, if they are unable to identify the site during joint inspection along with Bhuvi Realtech. In such scenario, Bhuvi Realtech will deduct Rs. 5,000 towards inspection charges and refund balance amount to the customer after due review and acceptance.
  • Bhuvi Realtech, will review the cancellation requests and arrange refunds with in 15 working days from acceptance of cancellation request.
  • Bhuvi Realtech’s decision regarding the cancellation will be final and binding on the customer.

Refund Process

  • Bhuvi Realtech acts as a facilitator on behalf of the customer. Bhuvi Realtech cannot be held responsible for any delays attributable to outside agencies such as banks, government departments etc. All activities are done an on best effort basis only. Bhuvi Realtech’s decision will be final and binding on this matter.
  • All Refunds will be processed and paid within 15 working days from the time the cancellation is accepted by the company. However actual timelines for refund to take place and for the credit to reach your account would depend on the speed of banking channels and the credit card companies involved in the transaction (as may be applicable)

Applicability

  • This is the general refund policy applicable for the services offered by the company. However, company may choose to have specific and individual refund policy for some of the services they offer. In such case individual service refund policy shall supersede this policy to the extent of any contrary mentioned in the individual policy.
